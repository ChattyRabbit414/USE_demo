USE Demos

Here, we showcase examples of Universal Sound Separation, Speech-Music Separation, and Target Sound Extraction (TSE). We provide the Mixture audio files and the separated Source audio files. We also include spectrograms for a more intuitive visual observation of the audio characteristics.

A quick guide to the labels:

  • Mix: Represents the mixed audio.
  • s: Denotes a ground truth source.
  • p: Denotes a predicted source.

Please note: Our USE framework predicts sources without prior knowledge of the actual number of sound sources. Therefore, you might observe a difference in the quantity of predicted audio files compared to the actual (ground truth) audio files in some instances.
